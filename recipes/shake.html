Iced Mocha Fusion Shake

Description

A delicious and cold 'milkshake' using a mocha flavored coffee mix.

Ingredients

  • Â¾ cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 3 tablespoons mocha flavored instant coffee mix
  • 1 cup crushed ice

Steps

  1. In a blender or food processor, combine milk, vanilla, sugar, coffee powder and crushed ice.
  2. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.
